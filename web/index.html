Our Mission

Ocean Guardian AI is pioneering the use of artificial intelligence in marine conservation. Our machine learning models can identify marine species, detect illegal fishing activities, track plastic pollution, and predict coral bleaching events.

Founded in 2020, we've developed state-of-the-art computer vision models trained on millions of underwater images. Our technology is now deployed in research stations across 40+ countries.

Threats to Our Oceans

Understanding the challenges our AI systems help monitor and address.

Plastic Pollution

Over 8 million tons of plastic enter our oceans annually. Our AI tracks and maps pollution hotspots for targeted cleanup operations.

Ocean Warming

Rising temperatures cause coral bleaching and species migration. Our predictive models help researchers prepare response strategies.

Overfishing

Over 30% of fish stocks are overexploited. Our vessel tracking AI helps authorities enforce fishing quotas and protected areas.

Ocean Acidification

The ocean absorbs 30% of CO2 emissions. Our monitoring systems track pH changes to protect vulnerable shell-forming species.

Oil Spills

Oil spills devastate marine ecosystems. Our satellite AI provides early detection for rapid response and damage assessment.

Habitat Destruction

Coastal development destroys critical habitats. Our change detection algorithms monitor and alert authorities to illegal activities.
